Courses

BS Computer Science

A Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) is an undergraduate degree program that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of computer systems, programming languages, algorithms, software engineering, and the theoretical foundations of computing. This degree equips students with the knowledge and skills necessary to design, develop, and analyze software and computer systems, making them well-prepared for various roles in the tech industry.

BS Information Technology

Information Technology, often abbreviated as IT, refers to the utilization, development, and management of computer systems, software, and networks for storing, retrieving, transmitting, and manipulating data. IT professionals work with a variety of technologies to ensure that computer systems function efficiently and securely to meet the needs of businesses and organizations.

BS Criminology

A Bachelor of Science in Criminology (BSCrim) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study of crime, criminal behavior, law enforcement, criminal justice, and the legal system. This degree equips students with knowledge and skills in crime prevention, criminal investigation, law enforcement administration, and social science research.

BS Nursing

A Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) is a rigorous undergraduate degree program designed to prepare students for a career in nursing. BSN programs provide a comprehensive education in both the theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed to excel in the healthcare industry.

BS Social Work

A Bachelor of Science in Social Work (BSSW) is an undergraduate degree program that prepares students for entry-level positions in social work and related fields. It equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to work with individuals, families, and communities to improve their well-being.

BS Islamic Studies

A Bachelor of Science in Islamic Studies (BS Islamic Studies) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the academic study of Islam, its teachings, history, culture, and contributions to various fields. This program provides students with a deep understanding of Islamic theology, jurisprudence, philosophy, and ethics. It also explores the historical and contemporary aspects of Islam, allowing students to critically analyze Islamic texts and traditions.

AB Asian Studies

A Bachelor of Science in Asian Studies (BS Asian Studies) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the interdisciplinary study of the cultures, societies, languages, history, politics, and economies of Asian countries and regions. This program provides students with a deep understanding of Asia's diverse and complex dynamics, preparing them for careers in fields such as international relations, diplomacy, business, education, and research.

BS Biology

A Bachelor of Science in Biology (BS Biology) is an undergraduate degree program that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the biological sciences. It covers a wide range of topics, including genetics, ecology, physiology, evolution, and cellular biology.

BS Architecture

A Bachelor of Science in Architecture (BS Architecture) is an undergraduate degree program that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the principles and practices of architecture. This program combines artistic, technical, and theoretical knowledge to prepare students for careers in the field of architecture and related disciplines.

BS Agriculture Biosystem Engineering

A Bachelor of Science in Agriculture Biosystem Engineering (BS ABSE) is an undergraduate degree program that combines principles from agricultural engineering, biological systems, and environmental science. This interdisciplinary field focuses on designing and implementing efficient and sustainable agricultural systems and processes.

BS Computer Engineering

A Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BS Computer Engineering) is an undergraduate degree program that integrates the principles of computer science and electrical engineering. This field focuses on the design, development, and maintenance of computer systems and their components.

BS Electronics Engineering

A Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering (BS Electronics Engineering) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the design, development, testing, and maintenance of electronic circuits and systems. It combines principles from electrical engineering and computer science to prepare students for careers in various fields related to electronics and technology.

BS Geodetic Engineering

A Bachelor of Science in Geodetic Engineering (BS Geodetic Engineering) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the measurement, representation, analysis, interpretation, and management of spatial and geographical information. Geodetic engineering involves the use of advanced technology to accurately measure and map the Earth's surface, making it a critical field for various applications, including land surveying, mapping, urban planning, and environmental monitoring.

BS Mechanical Engineering

A Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering (BSME) is an undergraduate degree program that equips students with a strong foundation in the principles of mechanical engineering. Mechanical engineering is a broad field that involves the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems.

BS Civil Engineering

A Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering (BSCE) is an undergraduate degree program that prepares students to plan, design, construct, and maintain infrastructure projects and systems. Civil engineering encompasses a wide range of activities, including the design of buildings, bridges, roads, dams, water supply systems, and environmental engineering projects.

BS Electrical Engineering

A Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering (BS Electrical Engineering or BSEE) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. Electrical engineering is a broad field that encompasses various sub-disciplines, including power systems, electronics, telecommunications, and control systems.

BS Environmental Engineering

A Bachelor of Science in Environmental Engineering (BS Environmental Engineering) is an undergraduate degree program that combines principles from various fields, including civil engineering, chemical engineering, biology, and chemistry. Environmental engineers focus on solving environmental problems and implementing sustainable solutions related to air and water quality, waste management, pollution control, and environmental conservation.

BS Industrial Engineering

A Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering (BSIE) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on optimizing processes and systems in various industries. Industrial engineers analyze, design, and implement integrated systems of people, materials, information, equipment, and energy to enhance efficiency and productivity.

BS Sanitary Engineering

A Bachelor of Science in Sanitary Engineering (BS Sanitary Engineering) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of sanitary systems and facilities. Sanitary engineers play a crucial role in ensuring the proper management of water supply, wastewater treatment, solid waste management, and environmental sanitation.
