Courses

BS Computer Science

A Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) is an undergraduate degree program that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of computer systems, programming languages, algorithms, software engineering, and the theoretical foundations of computing. This degree equips students with the knowledge and skills necessary to design, develop, and analyze software and computer systems, making them well-prepared for various roles in the tech industry.

BS Information Technology

Information Technology, often abbreviated as IT, refers to the utilization, development, and management of computer systems, software, and networks for storing, retrieving, transmitting, and manipulating data. IT professionals work with a variety of technologies to ensure that computer systems function efficiently and securely to meet the needs of businesses and organizations.

BS Criminology

A Bachelor of Science in Criminology (BSCrim) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study of crime, criminal behavior, law enforcement, criminal justice, and the legal system. This degree equips students with knowledge and skills in crime prevention, criminal investigation, law enforcement administration, and social science research.

BS Nursing

A Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) is a rigorous undergraduate degree program designed to prepare students for a career in nursing. BSN programs provide a comprehensive education in both the theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed to excel in the healthcare industry.

BS Social Work

A Bachelor of Science in Social Work (BSSW) is an undergraduate degree program that prepares students for entry-level positions in social work and related fields. It equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to work with individuals, families, and communities to improve their well-being.

BS Islamic Studies

A Bachelor of Science in Islamic Studies (BS Islamic Studies) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the academic study of Islam, its teachings, history, culture, and contributions to various fields. This program provides students with a deep understanding of Islamic theology, jurisprudence, philosophy, and ethics. It also explores the historical and contemporary aspects of Islam, allowing students to critically analyze Islamic texts and traditions.

About Us

What is CouGramTion?

CouGramTion is an innovative and intelligent course recommendation system designed to revolutionize the way students plan their academic journeys. Guided by cutting-edge machine learning algorithms, CouGramTion analyzes diverse data points, including academic performance and personal attributes, to predict and recommend tailored courses for incoming first-year students at Western Mindanao State University.

How it works?

By seamlessly integrating student data and advanced algorithms, CouGramTion accurately recommends the ideal courses for each student. Students simply sign up, provide their information, and CouGramTion does the rest. With CouGramTion, students can confidently embark on their educational paths, equipped with personalized course recommendations that align with their interests, strengths, and aspirations.
